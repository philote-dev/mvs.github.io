About Miami Vex Support

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Miami Vex Support was founded to provide engineering education and robotics opportunities to underrepresented communities in Miami. We bridge the resource gap that prevents talented students from accessing STEM education and competition opportunities.

Our Approach

Through partnerships with schools, volunteer mentors, and industry professionals, we've built a sustainable ecosystem focused on:

  • Establishing robotics programs at schools with equipment and curriculum
  • Organizing summer programs introducing engineering fundamentals
  • Building a network of volunteer mentors from colleges and industry
  • Supporting teams in preparing for VEX Robotics competitions

Vision for the Future

We're expanding to more schools, increasing summer program capacity, and creating pathways for students to continue STEM education through college. Our goal is sustainable impact that changes trajectories, not just provides experiences.
